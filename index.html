About Us

Med Innova Nepal is designed as a common platform for medical students to advance medical research, innovation, and technology, and to share these contributions globally for the benefit of low- and middle-income countries (LMICs) and underserved communities. It fosters the exchange of knowledge and research worldwide, with the aim of bridging gaps in skills and education between developed and developing nations. The initiative also emphasizes empowering medical and surgical knowledge, practical skills, simulation-based learning, and supporting sustainable developmental goals.
